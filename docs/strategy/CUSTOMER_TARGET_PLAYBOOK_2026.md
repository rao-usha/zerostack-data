### Market Facts

- **8,030+ single family offices globally**, $3.1T AUM (projected 10,720 by 2030)

- PE allocations rising to **27% of family office portfolios**, overtaking hedge funds and RE

- They do PE-style deals but lack PE-grade tools
- Can't afford (or can't justify) $25K+/seat PitchBook
