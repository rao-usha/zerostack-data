## 7. Analyst Reports on "PE Tech" / "DealTech" Market

### Key Reports

1. **Bain Global PE Report 2026** — Deal value surged 40% in 2025 to $4.9T. 49% of dealmakers use AI tools daily. 78% of PE firms prioritize AI-enhanced deal sourcing.

2. **McKinsey Global Private Markets Report 2026** — Total private markets AUM reached $16-16.5T (traditional + alternatives). Dry powder is elevated with 40%+ available for 2+ years.

3. **BlackRock/Preqin TAM Sizing** — Private markets data is an $8B TAM growing 12%/yr to $18B by 2030. This is the most cited market sizing figure in the industry.

4. **McKinsey "Private Market Visibility Gap"** — $4.3T valuation discrepancy caused by limited private company data access (cited in Datasite/Grata deal).

### Industry Dynamics

- **32,000 unsold companies** worth $3.8T (the "liquidity logjam") — Bain 2026
- **16,000+ companies** held for 4+ years (52% of buyout inventory, highest ever)
- **M&A deal value** hit $4.9T in 2025 (second-highest ever)
- Average buyout fund faces "most difficult fundraising period ever" (Bain)

## 8. Nexdata TAM Summary

### Directly Addressable Market

| Segment | TAM | Nexdata Relevance |
|---------|-----|-------------------|
| Private markets data (BlackRock est.) | $8B today, $18B by 2030 | Core: PE intelligence, fund data, company data |
| Alternative data for PE/hedge funds | $2.5-15B | Core: job postings, web scraping, SEC filings |
| Location intelligence | $25-27B | Site intelligence module |
| Family office data/software | $4B | Family office discovery + intelligence |
| PE CRM / dealtech tools | $2-3B (est.) | Deal sourcing, DD automation |

### Nexdata Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM)

**Target: Mid-market PE firms ($500M-$10B AUM)**
- Estimated 1,500-3,000 firms globally
- At $24K-$60K/yr (below PitchBook/Preqin pricing): **$36M - $180M SAM**

**Target: Family offices with direct investment programs**
- Estimated 3,000-5,000 family offices doing direct deals
- At $12K-$36K/yr: **$36M - $180M SAM**

**Target: Site selection / CRE data buyers**
- Estimated 5,000-10,000 potential accounts (developers, REITs, PE real estate)
- At $12K-$24K/yr: **$60M - $240M SAM**

### Total SAM: $130M - $600M

### Competitive Positioning

| Competitor | Price Point | Weakness Nexdata Exploits |
|-----------|------------|--------------------------|
| PitchBook | $12K-$40K/user/yr | No alternative data, no AI analyst, no site intel |
| Preqin | $25K-$80K/yr | Fund-level only, no company-level intelligence |
| Grata/Datasite | $5K-$15K/yr (est.) | Deal sourcing only, no DD automation or alt data |
| Capital IQ | $12K-$30K/user/yr | Public markets focus, weak on private companies |
| AlphaSense | $10K-$20K/seat/yr | Research/transcripts only, no PE workflow |

**Nexdata value prop: "Bloomberg Terminal for private markets with an AI analyst team built in" at $2K-$10K/mo vs. PitchBook at $25K+/seat/yr**
